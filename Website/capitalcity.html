About Capital City RP

Welcome to Capital City RP | CCRP, a next generation Georgia based FiveM roleplay server focused on realism, structure, and Southern charm.

Whether you're a civilian living your daily life, an officer protecting the community, or any role in between, Capital City RP offers an immersive Grand Theft Auto V roleplay experience that emphasizes authentic interactions and realistic scenarios.
